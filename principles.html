Guidelines
  1. Please! Take care of all the kids!
  2. Don't compare a child with another child. Every child is unique
  3. Don't compare time or result. Every child faces inexplicable challenges!
  4. Motivate them to practice everyday at the same time!
  5. They will gradually move up, don't need to push them too much!
  6. Please exercise care, encouragement of a kid, shouldn't demotivate other kids!
